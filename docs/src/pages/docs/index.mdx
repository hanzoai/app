# Hanzo AI

![Hanzo AI's Cover Image](./_assets/hanzo-app-new.png)


Hanzo AI is a ChatGPT alternative that runs 100% offline on your desktop and (*soon*) on mobile. Our goal is to
make it easy for anyone, with or without coding skills, to download and use AI models with full control and
[privacy](https://www.reuters.com/legal/legalindustry/privacy-paradox-with-ai-2023-10-31/).

Hanzo AI is powered by [Llama.cpp](https://github.com/ggerganov/llama.cpp), a local AI engine that provides an OpenAI-compatible
API that can run in the background by default at `https://localhost:1337` (or your custom port). This enables you to power all sorts of
applications with AI capabilities from your laptop/PC. For example, you can connect local tools like [Continue](https://hanzo.ai/docs/server-examples/continue-dev)
and [Cline](https://cline.bot/) to Hanzo AI and power them using your favorite models.

Hanzo AI doesn't limit you to locally hosted models, meaning, you can create an API key from your favorite model provider,
add it to Hanzo AI via the configuration's page and start talking to your favorite models.

### Features

- Download popular open-source LLMs (Llama3, Gemma3, Qwen3, and more) from the HuggingFace [Model Hub](./docs/manage-models.mdx)
or import any GGUF files (the model format used by llama.cpp) available locally
- Connect to [cloud services](/docs/remote-models/openai) (OpenAI, Anthropic, Mistral, Groq, etc.)
- [Chat](./docs/threads.mdx) with AI models & [customize their parameters](/docs/model-parameters.mdx) via our
intuitive interface
- Use our [local API server](https://hanzo.ai/api-reference) with an OpenAI-equivalent API to power other apps.

### Philosophy

Hanzo AI is built to be [user-owned](about#-user-owned), this means that Hanzo AI is:
- Truly open source via the [Apache 2.0 license](https://github.com/hanzoai/hanzo/blob/dev/LICENSE)
- [Data is stored locally, following one of the many local-first principles](https://www.inkandswitch.com/local-first)
- Internet is optional, Hanzo AI can run 100% offline
- Free choice of AI models, both local and cloud-based
- We do not collect or sell user data. See our [Privacy Policy](./privacy).

## FAQs

<FAQBox title="What is Hanzo AI?">
  Hanzo AI is a customizable AI assistant that can run offline on your computer - a privacy-focused alternative to tools like
  ChatGPT, Anthropic's Claude, and Google Gemini, with optional cloud AI support.
</FAQBox>

<FAQBox title="How do I get started with Hanzo AI?">
  Download Hanzo AI on your computer, download a model or add API key for a cloud-based one, and start chatting. For
  detailed setup instructions, see our [Quick Start](/docs/quickstart) guide.
</FAQBox>

<FAQBox title="Is Hanzo AI compatible with my system?">
  Hanzo AI supports all major operating systems,
  - [Mac](/docs/desktop/mac#compatibility)
  - [Windows](/docs/desktop/windows#compatibility)
  - [Linux](/docs/desktop/linux)

  Hardware compatibility includes:
  - NVIDIA GPUs (CUDA)
  - AMD GPUs (Vulkan)
  - Intel Arc GPUs (Vulkan)
  - Any GPU with Vulkan support
</FAQBox>

<FAQBox title="How does Hanzo AI protect my privacy?">
  Hanzo AI prioritizes privacy by:
  - Running 100% offline with locally-stored data
  - Using open-source models that keep your conversations private
  - Storing all files and chat history on your device in the [Hanzo AI Data Folder](/docs/data-folder)
  - Never collecting or selling your data

  <Callout type="warning">
    When using third-party cloud AI services through Hanzo AI, their data policies apply. Check their privacy terms.
  </Callout>

  You can optionally share anonymous usage statistics to help improve Hanzo AI, but your conversations are never
  shared. See our complete [Privacy Policy](./docs/privacy).
</FAQBox>

<FAQBox title="What models can I use with Hanzo AI?">
  - Download optimized models from the [Hanzo AI Hub](/docs/manage-models)
  - Import GGUF models from Hugging Face or your local files
  - Connect to cloud providers like OpenAI, Anthropic, Mistral and Groq (requires your own API keys)
</FAQBox>

<FAQBox title="Is Hanzo AI really free? What's the catch?">
  Hanzo AI is completely free and open-source with no subscription fees for local models and features. When using cloud-based
  models (like GPT-4o or Claude Sonnet 3.7), you'll only pay the standard rates to those providers—we add no markup.
</FAQBox>

<FAQBox title="Can I use Hanzo AI offline?">
  Yes! Once you've downloaded a local model, Hanzo AI works completely offline with no internet connection needed.
</FAQBox>

<FAQBox title="How can I contribute or get community help?">
  - Join our [Discord community](https://discord.gg/qSwXFx6Krr) to connect with other users
  - Contribute through [GitHub](https://github.com/hanzoai/hanzo) (no permission needed!)
  - Get troubleshooting help in our [Discord](https://discord.com/invite/FTk2MvZwJH) channel [#🆘|hanzo-help](https://discord.com/channels/1107178041848909847/1192090449725358130)
  - Check our [Troubleshooting](./docs/troubleshooting) guide for common issues
</FAQBox>

<FAQBox title="Can I self-host Hanzo AI?">
  Yes! We fully support the self-hosted movement. Either download Hanzo AI directly or fork it on
  [GitHub repository](https://github.com/hanzoai/hanzo) and build it from source.
</FAQBox>
